2026

  1. ROTATE: regret-driven open-ended training for ad hoc teamwork
    Caroline Wang*, Arrasy Rahman*, Jiaxun Cui, and 2 more authors
    Preprint, 2026
    paper abs

    Yoonchang Sung*, Shahaf S. Shperberg*, Qi Wang*, and 2 more authors
    Preprint, 2026
    paper abs

    TL;DR: A new class of TAMP problems is introduced by considering deadline constraints, where we prove its hardness and design efficient policy heuristics.

Before 2026

  1. PRESTO: fast motion planning using diffusion models based on key-configuration environment representation
    Mingyo Seo*, Yoonyoung Cho*, Yoonchang Sung, and 3 more authors
    IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA), 2025
    paper project abs

    TL;DR: This work addresses the question of what constitutes the ideal representation for solving motion planning problems as sampling from a diffusion model.

  2. Asynchronous task plan refinement for multi-robot task and motion planning
    Yoonchang Sung, Rahul Shome, and Peter Stone
    IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA), 2024
    paper talk abs

    TL;DR: A new multi-robot task and motion planning formulation that eliminates the need for pre-discretization of the space and synchronous actions among robots.

  3. Motion planning (in)feasibility detection using a prior roadmap via path and cut search
    Yoonchang Sung and Peter Stone
    Robotics: Science and Systems (RSS), 2023
    paper poster talk slides abs

    TL;DR: Iterative pathfinding and cut finding effectively reduce the search space, making the determination of whether a prior roadmap contains a feasible solution more efficient.

  4. A survey of decision-theoretic approaches for robotic environmental monitoring
    Yoonchang Sung, Zhiang Chen, Jnaneshwar Das, and 1 more author
    Foundations and Trends in Robotics, 2023
    paper abs

    TL;DR: A comprehensive survey on robotic environmental monitoring, covering environmental representations and properties, tasks, and decision-theoretic tools.

  5. Learning to correct mistakes: backjumping in long-horizon task and motion planning
    Yoonchang Sung*, Zizhao Wang*, and Peter Stone
    Conference on Robot Learning (CoRL), 2022
    paper poster abs

    TL;DR: A heuristic leveraged by supervised learning to guide task-level search, alleviating the need for consistently employing expensive backtracking.

  6. Towards optimal correlational object search
    Kaiyu Zheng, Rohan Chitnis, Yoonchang Sung, and 2 more authors
    IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA), 2022
    paper abs

    TL;DR: Leveraging correlational information among objects allows the POMDP formulation to efficiently discover optimal solutions for object search.

  7. Learning when to quit: meta-reasoning for motion planning
    IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems (IROS), 2021
    paper abs awarded

    Finalist for Best Cognitive Robotics Paper Award

    TL;DR: Being able to trade-off between plan quality and computation time lets you build a more efficient planner.

  8. Multi-resolution POMDP planning for multi-object search in 3D
    Kaiyu Zheng, Yoonchang Sung, George Konidaris, and 1 more author
    IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems (IROS), 2021
    paper abs awarded

    Winner of Best Robocup Paper Award

    TL;DR: The challenge of intractable partially observable 3D object search can be alleviated through a multi-resolution belief space representation.

  9. Reactive task and motion planning under temporal logic specifications
    Shen Li*, Daehyung Park*, Yoonchang Sung*, and 2 more authors
    IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA), 2021
    paper abs

    TL;DR: When robots work with humans, linear temporal logic allows the modeling of human interventions, while a behavior tree enables the robot to execute reactively.

  10. Environmental hotspot identification in limited time with a UAV equipped with a downward-facing camera

    IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA), 2021
    paper abs

  11. GM-PHD filter for searching and tracking an unknown number of targets with a mobile sensor with limited FOV
    Yoonchang Sung and Pratap Tokekar
    IEEE Transactions on Automation Science and Engineering (T-ASE), 2021
    IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA), 2017
    paper abs

  12. Game tree search for minimizing detectability and maximizing visibility

    IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA), 2019
    paper abs

  13. Distributed assignment with limited communication for multi-robot multi-target tracking

  15. Bayesian estimation based real-time fire-heading in smoke-filled indoor environments using thermal imagery
